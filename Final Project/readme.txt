Technical Requirements

Project contains 3 pages or sections (no more than 7) dedicated to your topic.
At least 3 subcategories (meaning, menu items such as About, Products, Gallery, Contact).
Your pages must have unique titles (unless it's a 1-page design). 
Images and text on every page.
External CSS that controls layout
Site functions in all browsers (Firefox, Chrome, Safari and Edge)
Incorporate some of the topics we've covered in class:
Appropriate meta tags (if not using Codepen).
Consistency in design from page to page or section to section.
Designed for your target audience.
One page containing a form or a table.
Optional: Specialty fonts. 
Optional: Multimedia. 
NOTE: * If you complete your final project fulfilling all the technical requirements, that is a passing grade, and it doesn't necessarily mean that you will be getting the highest grade. Pay close attention to all the requirements, not just technical. For the grade of A your website has to be stellar based on the design principles we cover in this class.
